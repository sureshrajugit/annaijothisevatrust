Goal: ₹ 10,00,000

With your support, we aim to build a well-equipped facility that will provide

  • Safe and Comfortable Living Spaces: The new building will offer spacious and comfortable living quarters for the children, ensuring they have a nurturing and secure home.
  • Educational Facilities: We are committed to providing quality education. The new building will include classrooms, a library, and computer labs to enhance their learning experience.
  • Healthcare Facilities: Access to healthcare is crucial. The new facility will have an infirmary to provide immediate medical attention when needed.
  • Recreational Areas: Children need space to play and develop their physical and social skills. We will create outdoor play areas and indoor recreational spaces.
  • Vocational Training: As children grow into young adults, we want to equip them with skills for a brighter future. The new facility will have vocational training workshops.
