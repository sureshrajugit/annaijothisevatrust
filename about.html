Who We Are

Annai Jothi Seva Trust was founded with a vision to bring a ray of hope into the lives of orphaned children. Established 1994, we have been a home to countless children, offering them a safe haven filled with love, education, and opportunities.

We Make A Difference By Providing Opportunities So That These People Can Have A Helping Hand To Improve Their Lives
