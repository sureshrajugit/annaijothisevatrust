Register yourself as
our volunteer.

Our special school is dedicated to providing education, support, and care to children and young adults with diverse abilities and special needs. We understand the profound impact that volunteers can have on the lives of our students, and we invite you to join our dedicated team.

  • Make a Meaningful Impact: Your time and skills can truly change lives. By volunteering, you'll contribute to the personal growth and development of our exceptional students.
  • Learn and Grow: Volunteering with us offers a unique opportunity to gain valuable experience and knowledge in the field of special education and inclusion.
  • Create Lasting Relationships: You'll be part of a supportive and caring community that values the contributions of each and every volunteer.
  • Flexible Commitment: We understand that your schedule may be busy, and we offer flexible volunteering options to accommodate your availability.

Our commitment is to nurture not just their physical needs but also their emotional, educational, and social well-being.
